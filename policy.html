Introduction

Melano is committed to protecting the privacy of its customers and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect your personal information.

Information Collection

Melano will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to any third parties unless required by law. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers, such as payment processors and shipping carriers, who assist us in providing our services.

Personal Information: We may collect your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and other personal information when you purchase, create an account or sign up for our email list.

Payment Information: We may collect payment information, such as your credit card number and billing information, when you purchase on our website.

Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of our website, such as the pages you visit and the products you purchase.

Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our website, such as the type of device, its operating system, and browser type.


Information Use

Melano uses the information we collect to provide you with the best possible customer experience, including:

  • Sending marketing and promotional communications, such as our email newsletter.
  • Customizing your shopping experience.
  • Protecting against fraud and illegal activities.

Information Sharing

Melano will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to any third parties unless required by law. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers, such as payment processors and shipping carriers, who assist us in providing our services.

Data Security

Melano takes the security of your personal information seriously and implements appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. In addition, we regularly review and update our security practices to ensure that your information remains protected.

Cookies

Melano uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your website experience and collect information about your usage. Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit our website. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use some features of our website.

Melano reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently. If we change our Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
